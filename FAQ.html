    Answer: An Endolift® session typically takes about 60-90 minutes for a patient, depending on the areas being treated and the complexity of the procedure. For training sessions, which include detailed instruction and hands-on practice, the duration is longer, generally ranging from 4 to 6 hours to ensure comprehensive learning and skill development.

    Answer: The recovery time for an Endolift® treatment is minimal, as it is a non-surgical, minimally invasive procedure. Most patients can return to their normal daily activities immediately after the treatment. Some may experience mild swelling, redness, or bruising in the treated areas, which typically resolves within a few days. Full results gradually become noticeable over the following weeks as collagen production increases and the skin continues to tighten.
