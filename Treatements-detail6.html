Lower Eyelid Endolift

Lift, tighten, and refresh tired eyes—no needles, no scalpels.

Refresh your eyes—no needles, no surgery.

Lower Eyelid Endolift® targets under-eye bags and skin laxity using a precision micro-laser delivered from beneath the skin. It tightens, smooths, and stimulates collagen—all in one session, with no visible scars and minimal downtime. It’s a subtle, natural-looking solution for patients who want to look more rested without fillers or invasive procedures.

Why Patients Choose Endolift for the Under-Eye Area

We target fat pads and lax skin from the inside —without cutting the surface

We use a micro-fiber thinner than hair to deliver heat precisely where needed

No stitches, fillers, or visible trauma —perfect for delicate under-eye tissue

We perform the procedure in under 30 minutes, and most clients resume daily activities in 1–2 days

You'll see visible tightening and smoothing in weeks, with ongoing improvements for up to 12 months

What to Expect on Treatment Day

1

Consult & Prep

We evaluate the under-eye area and apply topical + local anesthetic.

2

Laser Activation

We insert a 200–300 μm fiber through a tiny puncture near the lower lash line.

  • • The laser gently melts small fat pockets
  • • Tightens skin by heating collagen fibers
  • • Triggers collagen regeneration for months
3

Wrap & Go

No stitches needed. You leave with cooling pads and full aftercare instructions.

Pre- and Post-Care

5 days before

Avoid aspirin, alcohol, and fish oil.

48 hours before

Stop the use of retinol, acids, or actives around the eyes.

Day 0–1

Apply cold compresses, avoid heat and rubbing.

Day 2–3

Resume light activity; swelling should decrease.

Day 4+

Gentle skincare and SPF are recommended; visible lifting continues to improve over the months.
